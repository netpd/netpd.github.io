Welcome to the home page of the Networked Systems Performance and Design Lab, led by Professor Konstantinos Psounis.


We are part of the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science departments at the University of Southern California. Our group is a diverse research group researching into a range of problems in systems and theory related to networking. We are particularly interested in modelling and analyzing the performance of the Internet, mobile ad-hoc networks, delay and disruptive tolerant networks, sensor networks, peer to peer networks, and the web. We are also very interested in designing methods, protocols, and algorithms to solve problems related to such systems. For a more detailed discussion on our research efforts, please see our publications and the fundeed projects.
